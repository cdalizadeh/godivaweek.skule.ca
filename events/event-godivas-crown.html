It’s that time of the year again - time for Lia Cod, Queen of Clubs, to crown a successor to represent the spirit of Lady Godiva! Come watch each contestant perform a short, creative and possibly entertaining act. Who will the judges and Lia crown as our next Queen of Hearts? Join us in the pit to find out! Don’t miss the chance to witness the new queen click her heels and yell out ‘Queen of Hearts’ for the first time!
