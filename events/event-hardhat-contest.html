FROSH HARD HAT CONTEST!

An event where, you, the Frosh, can show off your building and decorating skills right on your mighty Skule Hard Hat.

Come to the Pit at 6pm and strut your stuff for the judges, who will determine the coolest hard hats of your year! A few rules:

1. Make sure your hardhat is safe for everyone. The contest is indoors.

2. IF YOU PLAN TO USE PYROTECHNICS OR FIRE PROJECTILES: Please notify the organizing committee so we can plan for moving outside, allowing you to demonstrate safely.

3. If your hard hat has sharp objects, cap them make sure that corners and sharp edges are well protected.

See you soon!
